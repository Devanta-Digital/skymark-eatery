#### Gruppo Terroni

Our mission at Gruppo Terroni has always been to bring authenticity and tradition to all the dishes on our menu. To do this, we emphasize the simplicity and quality of the ingredients we import from Italy. Materia prima, or the raw material, is the soul of our kitchen. We don’t cut any corners when it comes to materia prima and we never will. When it comes to tradition, we want to keep it going. While we stay true to time honored recipes and methods, we continue to evolve and innovate with time.

#### Gruppo Terroni

Our mission at Gruppo Terroni has always been to bring authenticity and tradition to all the dishes on our menu. To do this, we emphasize the simplicity and quality of the ingredients we import from Italy. Materia prima, or the raw material, is the soul of our kitchen. We don’t cut any corners when it comes to materia prima and we never will. When it comes to tradition, we want to keep it going. While we stay true to time honored recipes and methods, we continue to evolve and innovate with time.

[Our Story](https://www.terroni.com/our-story)

First time here? Scroll down to learn about our story

### Terroni Queen

1992

1992

The original location. Through its various expansions and renovations over the past 30 years, it is still the beating heart of Terroni and a neighborhood favorite.

### Terroni Victoria

1994

1994

Our second Terroni opened in the financial district on Victoria Street. Today, it is the home of La Bettola di Terroni.

### Terroni Balmoral

2000

2000

Our third Terroni goes uptown and served its last pizza in December 2010 before moving a few blocks south to Summerhill, also known as Terroni Price.

### Cavinona Wine Agency

2006

2006

We launch our wine agency to share our passion for Italian wine and introduce Canadians to lesser known grape varietals. Exclusive to us, we’ve handpicked more than 90 producers from all over Italy.

### Terroni LA Beverly

2007

2007

Terroni goes west. Our first location outside of Toronto opened with our partner Shereen in California and offers the same traditional Italian dining experience.

### Terroni Adelaide

2007

2007

Housed in the historic Toronto courthouse, Terroni Adelaide spans across four floors with a variety of rooms and spaces perfect for large group dining and private events of different sizes.

### Osteria Ciceri e Tria

2008

2008

When Terroni Victoria moved to Adelaide, we opened an osteria in its place. Offering a revolving fixed menu and specializing in appetizers.

### Bar Centrale

2010

2010

Bar Centrale is our wine bar inspired by the beautiful train stations in Italy and located directly below Terroni Price.

### La Bettola Toronto

2010

2010

We expanded the osteria and doubled up the kitchen to open La Bettola, our restaurant specializing in seasonality and mostly Sicilian cuisine.

### Terroni Price

2011

2011

In the heart of Summerhill, Price is the latest Terroni to open. Above Bar Centrale, Terroni Price offers two floors of dining and event experiences including our renowned rooftop patio.

### Sud Forno Queen

2013

2013

Our love for the art of bread making led us to opening Sud Forno, our first bakery neighbouring Terroni Queen. Here we serve bread, pastries, pizza stirata, panini, HMR, groceries, wine and more. Password: mother yeast.

### Terroni LA Downtown

2013

2013

We opened our second location in California in the up and coming area of downtown LA, located in a beautiful historic bank from the 1920s.

### La Bottega Online Shop

2016

2016

We launched our online store La Bottega to make it easier to order our artisanal in-house products, imported Italian goods, tickets for our events and more.

### Dopolavoro

2016

2016

Our specialty mini-market and wine shop in Los Angeles.

### Sud Forno Temperance

2017

2017

In yet another heritage building, the second Sud Forno is located in the heart of the financial district hosting our servery, La Mensa, for quick lunches and takeout and Sud Forno’s restaurant for a full dine-in experience.

### Spaccio East

2019

2019

Spaccio is our commissary kitchen designed to service all of our restaurants and bakeries ensuring quality and consistency. In time, it developed into a caffè, grocer, and restaurant.

### Stock T.C

2020

2020

Stock T.C is a joint venture between ourselves and boutique butcher, Cumbraes. It consists of a grocery store and kitchen on the first floor and Stock Bar on the upper two floors, a restaurant serving the best of both worlds.

### Porta

2021

2021

In the depths of lockdown life, we launch Porta, Toronto’s first frozen Italian subscription delivery service.

### Spaccio West

2022

2022

Our latest Spaccio opened on the west end to provide even more support to our ever-growing operation.

### Terroni Sterling

2023

2023

Immerse yourself in all things Italian at this Terroni - Spaccio hybrid location. Terroni Sterling offers a full-service Terroni restaurant alongside the convenience of a Spaccio grocer and café.

### La Bettola LA

2023

2023

Think of La Bettola as the greatest hits of Terroni combined with unique dishes largely inspired by food seasonality.
