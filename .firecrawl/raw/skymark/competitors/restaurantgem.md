## Where cultural heritage meets culinary excellence

We layer Surinamese-Hindustani spices, French precision and Dutch seasonal produce into dishes that feel both familiar and daring. Every plate tells a story and ends with a smile.

![](https://restaurantgem.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Ingang-deur-restaurant-GEM.webp)![](https://restaurantgem.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Restaurant-GEM-trap-wijnkast.jpg)![](https://restaurantgem.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/U0A0396-scaled.jpg)

## Around the world, one plate at a time

Our six-course journey menu traces Soenil’s path from Paramaribo to Provence, finished with Brabant warmth and paired wines poured at the perfect moment.
