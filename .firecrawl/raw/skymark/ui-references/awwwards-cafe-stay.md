# [A Café You Stay With for a While](https://interactive-cafe-store.vercel.app/)

- [![](https://assets.awwwards.com/awards/media/cache/thumb_user_70/avatar/2785260/698704d10cc76747001380.png)**eter-wang**](https://www.awwwards.com/eter-wang/)


- [![](https://assets.awwwards.com/awards/submissions/2026/02/6984a060099d0170574212.png)](https://interactive-cafe-store.vercel.app/)

### This is a small interactive café experience designed around stillness, curiosity, and gentle discovery.  Visitors can explore the space at their own pace — opening the menu, approaching the cake cabinet, and selecting a cake to eat, one bite at a time.  Each interaction is intentionally quiet. There are no instructions, no scores, and no urgency. Cakes slowly disappear, backgrounds subtly change, and sounds appear only at the very end of an action.  The project focuses on creating a calm, tactile digital space where interaction feels more like spending time than completing tasks.
