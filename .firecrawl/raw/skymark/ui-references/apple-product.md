## Privacy

### Your data.   Just where you want it.

#### Groundbreaking privacy protections.

Apple Intelligence is integrated into your iPhone through on-device processing. With Private Cloud Compute, it can draw on larger, Apple-designed server-based models, running on Apple silicon, to handle more complex requests while protecting your privacy.

![Hand holding iPhone 17 showing all screen display, lock screen showing Liquid Glass border](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone/home/cj/images/overview/consider_modals/privacy/modal_privacy__e7dubfc08ywm_large.jpg)

#### Sign in safely.

The Passwords app makes it easier to access account passwords, passkeys, Wi‑Fi passwords, and two-factor authentication codes stored securely on your device or synced with iCloud Keychain.

![iPhone screen showing Passwords menu with options All, Passkeys, Codes, Wi-Fi, Security, Deleted](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone/home/cj/images/overview/consider_modals/privacy/modal_sign_in__bvqm1dvv5uqa_large.jpg)

#### Browse securely.

Private Browsing in Safari locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading on pages, and removes tracking added to URLs as you browse.

![A person holding iPhone16 in pink finish in front of her face showing back exterior, dual camera system on left top corner.](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone/home/cj/images/overview/consider_modals/privacy/modal_browse__cmw3m6uol8b6_large.jpg)

#### Share privately.

All messages sent with iMessage use end-to-end encryption between devices, so they’re only seen by those you send them to. And Mail Privacy Protection hides your IP address, so senders can’t determine your location.

![iPhone Screen showing pop-up messages](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone/home/cj/images/overview/consider_modals/privacy/modal_share__eguowu1jbus2_large.jpg)
