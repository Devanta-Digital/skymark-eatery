# Skymark vs reference set — condensed comparison

Sources: raw scrapes under `.firecrawl/raw/skymark/competitors/` and `.firecrawl/raw/skymark/ui-references/`.

## Premium Toronto-area / national peers

| Brand | What they do better | What Skymark should NOT copy |
|-------|---------------------|------------------------------|
| **Alo / Restaurant Gem** | Extreme restraint, cinematic pacing, fewer CTAs, editorial typography | Ultra-minimal navigation hiding utility |
| **Mamakas / Damas** | Strong cultural identity and **distinctive** photography | Heavy ethnic theming that is not Skymark’s story |
| **Terroni** | Confident Italian **heritage** + operational scale cues | Corporate chain sameness; losing “neighbourhood kitchen” warmth |

## Non-restaurant UI references (structure only)

| Source | Borrow | Avoid |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Apple / Stripe** | Section **resets**, confident spacing, scannable bullets | Cold tech minimalism, hiding contact |
| **Framer / galleries** | Asymmetric grids, **one** dramatic layout moment | Experimental nav, slow JS |
| **Awwwards restaurant cases** | Hero composition ideas, scroll-linked depth (subtle) | Scroll-jank, novelty for novelty |

## Skymark’s wedge

**“Premium but efficient”** — Italian warmth + **Pearson-adjacent office utility** + **catering credibility**. Competitors win on spectacle; Skymark should win on **trust, clarity, and appetite**.
