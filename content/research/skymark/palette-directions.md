# Skymark — three non-generic palette directions (evidence-led)

Reference scrapes live under `.firecrawl/raw/skymark/competitors/` and `ui-references/`. Below: **tendencies** inferred from those brands + Apple/Stripe pacing notes (not pixel-perfect steals).

## A — Dark editorial / high-contrast (Damas, Restaurant GEM, Terroni evening)

- **Surfaces:** Near-black to charcoal base (`~220–260°` hue, very low L), warm text (`#f2eadf` family), **sharp** light/dark section flips.
- **Contrast:** Large type on dark; thin rules in `white/10–15%`; imagery does heavy lifting.
- **Accent:** Single **brass/gold** line or kicker; **wine/crimson** CTA (not dusty terracotta).
- **Reject:** Beige body wash, same L everywhere.

## B — Bright Mediterranean / sunlit premium (Mamakas, coastal Italian)

- **Surfaces:** High L paper white, **cool** shadow (`slate`/`blue-gray`), occasional **sun-washed** warm panel (one per page max).
- **Contrast:** Deep teal or deep olive for type; **saturated tomato** CTA.
- **Risk on mobile:** Glare + weak CTA if white dominates; needs disciplined dark type.

## C — Sharp modern Italian retail (Apple/Stripe hierarchy + strong chroma)

- **Surfaces:** Neutral gray ramp + **one** loud accent (flag red or racing green) on white.
- **Contrast:** Tight type scale, lots of negative space, **bold** primary buttons.
- **Risk:** Can read “QSR app” if imagery is weak.

## vs current Skymark (rejected pass)

- Current: **warm beige ramp** + muted terracotta + soft shadows → reads as generic “AI hospitality.”
- Weak **hierarchy** between chrome, hero, and sections (similar mid-tones).

## Ranking (browser impact × mobile usability × catering credibility)

1. **A** — Best **instant** premium read; CTAs pop on dark; logo reads clearly with a light-on-dark variant; avoids beige-on-beige.
2. **C** — Strong for clarity; needs excellent photography to avoid retail-generic.
3. **B** — Beautiful when lit well; hardest to keep **catering** feeling urgent on small screens.

## Selected direction: **A (dark editorial)**

Reason: strongest **section reset** and **CTA separation**, aligns with reference set (GEM/Terroni/Damas mood), fixes “flat template” without relying on more beige.
