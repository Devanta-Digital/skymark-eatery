# Image & art direction — Skymark

## Creative brief (one shoot, one grade)

- Warm natural light, shallow depth, **slightly moody** shadow (not HDR gloss).
- **Terracotta and olive** should appear as **environment** tones, not as filters slapped on everything.
- **No third-party logos** (delivery apps, beverage brands) in frame.
- **Text-safe** hero crops: keep subject biased to one vertical third for headline lockup.

## Shot list priorities

1. Full-bleed **hero** pasta or steam-over-plate moment.
2. **Catering tray** spread with depth and negative space.
3. **Sandwich texture** close-up for menu emotional anchor.
4. **Counter rhythm** (hands, tickets, steam) for “real business” credibility.
5. Optional: **office lunch** context — restrained, no cheesy stock poses.

## Technical

- Ratios: hero **16:9**; cards **4:3**; `object-cover`; fixed aspect wrappers to prevent CLS.
- If an asset is good but busy: **crop tighter**, darken edge, add subtle vignette in CSS (already prefer grain hero).

## Do not

- Bright white seamless “catalog” backgrounds.
- Mixed colour temperatures across the homepage grid.
